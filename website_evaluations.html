Website Evaluations

Apple

The Apple website is a great example of clean and effective web design, featuring high contrast, strong alignment, and well-placed elements.

  • Contrast: Strong contrast with white space and dark elements. The images and text stand out.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, buttons, and layout across the entire site.
  • Alignment: Everything is well-aligned with clear sections.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped, making navigation intuitive.
